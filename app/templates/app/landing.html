The Days


The Days is a to do list application. A simple task management application that allows you to keep track of your activities.
You can use this application as you please. Use it to make daily goals. Or a bucket list of things you want to do.
Once an activity is added it can be marked as completed and also, deleted. You can also undo an activity.

Create an account to get started. Click on the Sign Up link up top to create an account.
Once you have signed up you can login to begin listing!

Have a listacular time!
